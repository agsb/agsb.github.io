# Operations on 8-bit values

[7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0]
  
## rotate left
  
  [7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0] ==> [6][5][4][3][2][1][0][7]

## rotate right 

  [7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0] ==> [0][7][6][5][4][3][2][1]

## reverse mirror
  
  [7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0] ==> [0][1][2][3][4][5][6][7]

## nible exchange

  [7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0] ==>  [3][2][1][0][7][6][5][4]
  
## even odds  
  [7][6][5][4][3][2][1][0] ==> [7][5][3][1][6][4][2][0]
